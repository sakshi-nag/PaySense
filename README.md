# PaySense 💸

### Track everything. Feel every rupee.

PaySense is a behavioral finance application designed to help college students and young digital payment users become more aware of their spending habits.

Unlike traditional expense trackers, PaySense focuses on reducing unconscious overspending by reintroducing the “pain of paying” through real-time behavioral feedback after transactions.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Digital payments have made spending effortless—but also invisible.

As the psychological friction of physical cash disappears, users often lose awareness of their spending patterns, leading to unconscious overspending.

Most existing expense trackers only analyze spending after it happens rather than creating awareness at the moment of transaction.

---

## 💡 Solution

PaySense acts as a financial awareness layer on top of existing UPI apps by:

* Detecting UPI transactions via SMS
* Unifying spending data across multiple apps
* Delivering real-time spending feedback
* Encouraging mindful spending through behavioral insights
* Tracking budgets, goals, and spending patterns

## 🌟 Core Features

  * App source

## 🧠 Behavioral Finance Approach

PaySense is built on the principle that:

> Users don’t overspend because they want to—
> They overspend because they don’t feel the spend.

By introducing subtle, timely spending awareness, PaySense helps users make more conscious financial decisions.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. User completes payment via UPI
2. SMS transaction is detected
3. PaySense parses transaction data
4. Behavioral feedback popup appears
5. User confirms or edits
6. Transaction is stored
7. Dashboard and insights update
